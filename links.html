Net Neighbors

Here are some artists that I like interacting with on different parts of the web that also have a presence on the indie web!

In the wise words of Melon:
Is the website a dead end? If it is, it's a dead weight for the web!
Message or mention me on Mastodon or Discord if you'd like to become net neighbors!

Jazz

Jazz draws a lot of really cool stuff, from fantasy isopods to sci-fi vehicles and everything in between!

Dave Pagurek

Dave is a programmer and artist that works on tools for computer graphics, including but not limited to WebGL mode in p5.js.

uuupah

uuupah is a very talented character artist that works both digitally and traditionally.

Clockwork

Clockwork is a writer creating stories imagining a better and more equal world and the realistic paths to achieve them. A true solarpunk! You can read their work on their site.

Discord Servers

Here are some Discord servers I like to hang out in!

Doodle Crew

Doodle Crew is an 18+ and safe for work community for artists looking to meet other artists, share their work, and grow together!

32-Bit Cafe

32-Bit Cafe is an 18+ and safe for work community for discussions related to webweaving, webcrafting, website-building hobby that focusing on self-expression and art without necessarily focusing on the "old web" aesthetic.

They also have tons of resources on their website, including a very comprehensive guide on creating your very own website!
