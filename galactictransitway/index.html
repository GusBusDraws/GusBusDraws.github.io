Galactic Transitway

The Galactic Transitway is a worldbuilding project of mine. The idea is based on a scifi infrastructure maintenence crew. The idea is that they'd travel the galaxy on adventures involving infrastructure, like the wormhole gates that connect the citizens of the Transitway!
