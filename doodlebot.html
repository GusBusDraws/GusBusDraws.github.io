DoodleBot

Since the invention of the pencil, people have asked the age old question: What should I draw?

Ask no longer! Or rather, ask DoodleBot!

DoodleBot is a prompt-generation bot that helps reduce friction when making art. Feel free to post any DoodleBot inspired creations on the subreddit r/DoodleBot!

Enter a prompt below, or use the command "!help" to learn how to generate a prompt. The command "!keywords" will list the available keywords that can be used.
