DoodleBot

Since the invention of the pencil, people have asked the age old question: What should I draw?

Ask no longer! Or rather, ask DoodleBot!

DoodleBot is a prompt-generation bot that helps reduce friction when making art. Feel free to post any DoodleBot inspired creations on the subreddit r/DoodleBot or tag @gusbusdraws if you post somewhere else!

Select keywords from the drop down menu below, then press "Generate prompt" to have DoodleBot give you a prompt from the structure. You can type other words between the keywords! Try pressing "Random prompt" to get a few examples.

Did you get a prompt that you really liked? Do you have a suggestion for a new keyword? Share in the comments below!
