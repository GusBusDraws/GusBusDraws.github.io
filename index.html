DoodleBot

Since the invention of the pencil, people have asked the age old question: What should I draw?

Ask no longer! Or rather, ask DoodleBot!

DoodleBot is a prompt-generation bot that helps reduce friction when making art. DoodleBot is currently available as a Python-powered Bot at in the Doodle Crew Discord server and on the subreddit r/DoodleBot.
